# ShowReady — Tester Feedback

Thanks for trying the ShowReady demo. Five minutes of your honest reaction is worth a lot. Please answer as many as you like and send them back (the **Send feedback** button in the demo will email these automatically).

## The essentials

1. **Would you use this before a private showing?** Why or why not?
2. **The verify step** (license + selfie + optional location): useful, unnecessary, or concerning? Anything you'd change?
3. **The agreement step** (auto-filling the SA-5): would this save you time? What's missing?
4. **Speed:** did the agent flow feel fast enough to do in a driveway? Did the buyer flow feel quick and clear?
5. **Trust:** anything that felt off, confusing, or that you wouldn't be comfortable asking a buyer to do?
